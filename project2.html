Frequently asked questions

How long will it take for my order to ship?

Upon purchasing an Ai Pin, you’ll be invited via email to set up your Humane Plan and account. Once complete, your Ai Pin will ship within 1-2 business days. You’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking details when your Ai Pin is on its way. To check the status, visit Orders within My Account.

Do you ship internationally?

We are currently only selling and shipping our products in the US. Within the US, we do not currently ship to PO boxes, military bases, or US territories. Sign up to be notified when Ai Pin becomes available in your market.

Can I order more than one Ai Pin?

Each Ai Pin is associated with a single, corresponding Humane account. Ordering multiple Ai Pins requires separate transactions and Humane accounts.

Do you accept returns?

We accept returns for a refund up to 90 days after your order ships. See our Return Policy for more information.

Can I use Ai Pin without paying for a plan?

An active Humane Plan is required to use Ai Pin. The Humane Plan includes your cloud data storage, access to Ai services, and a domestic cellular plan with talk, text, and data. We’ll guide you through the setup process before your Ai Pin ships. More details on paid add-ons can be found here.
